About BLAIS

Blais is a Billboard-charting producer and mixing engineer with a career rooted in hip hop's legendary sound. His production credits include two full-length albums for Bizzy Bone of Bone Thugs-n-Harmony: Carbon Monoxide and The Mantra.

Over the years, Blais has worked with some of the most respected names in rap and R&B, including Bone Thugs-n-Harmony, Do Or Die, Devin The Dude, and Snow Tha Product.

Beyond production, Blais has built a career as a skilled mixing engineer, shaping records with clarity and punch. His approach keeps vocals sharp, drums heavy, and melodies wide.

With a background that spans studio sessions, live production, and collaborations with both major-label artists and independent talent, Blais continues to push forward, delivering beats and mixes that stand the test of time.
